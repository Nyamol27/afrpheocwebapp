Join Us in Advocating for PHEOCs

At the WHO African EOC Network, we believe in the crucial role of PHEOCs in national emergency management. PHEOCs are essential for effectively managing emergency mitigation, preparedness, response, and recovery activities. We advocate for the integration of traditional public health services into a comprehensive emergency management model, ensuring preparedness and capacity building are at the forefront.
