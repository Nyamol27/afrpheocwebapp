01.
How do I access the online forum?

To access the online forum, simply log in to your account and navigate to the "Online Forum" section from the main menu. Here, you can view and participate in discussions on various topics.

02.
How can I view ongoing projects and related tasks in the technical working group?

You can view ongoing projects and related tasks by accessing the "Technical Working Group" section. Here, you'll find comprehensive details about projects, tasks, progress, and any related documentation.

03.
How do I submit feedback?

To submit feedback, go to the "Feedback" section and follow the prompts to share your thoughts, suggestions, or concerns. Your feedback is valuable in helping us improve the application.

04.
What is the purpose of the special module for Trainers?

The special module for Trainers is designed to facilitate communication and collaboration among trainers involved in strengthening the Public Health Emergency Operations Center (PHEOC) in the African region. It also allows trainers to manage their CVs and availability for deployment.

05.
How can trainers exchange their experiences in strengthening PHEOC in the African Region?

Trainers can exchange their experiences by participating in discussions within the dedicated forum or by initiating conversations with other trainers using the messaging feature provided in the Trainers module.

06.
How do trainers manage their CV and availability for deployment?

Trainers can manage their CVs and availability for deployment by accessing their profile settings within the Trainers module. Here, they can update their CV information, specify their availability for deployment.

07.
Can I access the application from any device?

Yes, the application is accessible from any device with internet connectivity. Whether you're using a computer, tablet, or smartphone, you can log in to the application using your credentials and access its features.

08.
Is there a notification system for updates and important announcements?

Yes, the application features a notification system that alerts users about updates, important announcements, new forum discussions, project milestones, and other relevant information. Users can customize their notification preferences to suit their needs.

9.
What should I do if I encounter technical issues or need assistance?

If you encounter technical issues or require assistance, you can reach out to our technical support team via the "Support" section or contact us directly through email or phone. Our support team is dedicated to resolving any issues and providing assistance to ensure a seamless user experience.
