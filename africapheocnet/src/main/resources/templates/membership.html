Africa PHEOC Network!

Our network is a diverse community of dedicated individuals, organizations, and institutions committed to strengthening public health emergency response across the African Region. Whether you are a Member State, a regional organization, a partner, an individual professional, or an academic institution, there's a place for you in our network.

Africa PHEOC-NET

Benefits of Membership

Being part of a network hosted by the WHO Health Emergencies Programme

Joining the WHO Health Emergencies Programme network empowers Member States to manage health crises and coordinate international responses effectively.
This collaboration ensures timely relief to affected populations and plays a pivotal role in PHEM across the African Region.

Networking and collaboration

The network facilitates collaboration among members, enhancing health security across Africa.
Through partnerships and cooperative efforts, members work together to address emerging health challenges and strengthen emergency response capabilities in the region.

Learning and mutual support

Members benefit from a supportive environment conducive to continuous learning, experience sharing, and innovation.
This fosters mutual support among members, enabling the exchange of best practices and insights to improve emergency preparedness and response efforts.

The Network resources and news

Access to the EOC website provides members with valuable resources and real-time updates on public health emergency operations.
This centralized platform serves as a hub for knowledge exchange and information sharing, enhancing the effectiveness of emergency response initiatives.
